✦ About the Course

Gita Primer

Learn Gita Live Gita's GITA PRIMER is a basic course on Bhagavad Gita As It Is, which is transcendental wisdom originally spoken by the Supreme Lord Sri Krishna to his dearmost devotee and friend Arjuna in the battlefield of Mahabharatha approximately 5000 years ago.

This course covers the essence of Bhagavad Gita in the form of 5 lessons comprising why we should learn Bhagavad Gita, the subject of consciousness and reincarnation, who is God and what does His kingdom look like, what is yoga and what are the various yoga systems, and finally what is the goal of human life and how to achieve it.

The aim of the course is to introduce key concepts and teachings of Bhagavad Gita as it is scientifically in a summarized manner with practical examples, for beginners to appreciate this ancient wisdom and be inspired to study this transcendental literature in detail as well as to follow Lord Sri Krishna’s teachings in their daily lives.

*Translation & Commentary by His Divine Grace A.C. Bhaktivedanta Swami Srila Prabhupada, Founder Acharya of ISKCON.
